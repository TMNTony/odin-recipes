Tatertot Hotdish

Description

Tatertot hotdish is my favorite midwestern casserole. It casn be made with or without cheese and can also sub out different vegetables according to your own taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 large onion
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • 1 bag frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1 32oz bag of tater tots
  • choice of seasoning

Directions

  1. Saute onion in large pan.
  2. Season ground beef and add to onions. Continue to brown beef until thuroughly cooked.
  3. In a large bowl, add the beef and onion mixture to the frozen vegetable and canned soup.
  4. Place mixture in a large 9x13 glass baking pan, and cover with forzen tots.
  5. Bake in oven at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  6. (Optional) cover finished casserole with shredded cheddar and broil until golden.
