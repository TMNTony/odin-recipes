Filet Mingon

Discription

Classic filet mingon recipe. Best paired with a vegetable and a starch.

ingredients

  • Filet of beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • Aromatics such as rosemary or thyme
  • Olive oil
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Sous vide steak in a vacuumed sealed bag along with your chosen aromatics at 130 degrees for 1 hour.
  2. Remove steak from bag and pat dry. Liberally apply salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a cast iron skillet to high and apply 1 tbsp of olive oil. Place steak in the pan.
  4. Put 1 tbsp of butter along with additional aromatics in pan.
  5. Spoon melted butter on steak. Turn steak and continue basting in butter.
  6. Remove steak from pan when both sides have desired crust.
