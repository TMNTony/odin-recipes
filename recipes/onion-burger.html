Onion Burger

Discription

This burger is very oniony, and is super easy to make.

ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • Sliced cheddar
  • Strong hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Garlic powder

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Set aside.
  2. Using a mandoline, slice onion very thin, and season onion with salt and pepper.
  3. Make patties out of beef, and place them onto hot pan. No additional oil is needed.
  4. Cover cooking patties with onion, and allow to cook for 5-8 minutes before flipping burger onion side down.
  5. While onion side down burger is cooking, place slices of cheddar on burger.
  6. WHike the burger conitunes to cook, hamburger bones can be toasted if you have an additional pan.
  7. Place cooked burgers on the toasted buns and enjoy.
