Cheese Omelette

Description

This is a simple cheese omelette recipe that I use for my daughter. THe ingredients and process are simple, timing is important to get the best texture.

ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Handfull shredded cheese (usually cheddar)
  • 1/2-1 tbsp of salterd butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs being carefull not to over work.
  2. In a heated skillet, add butter to melt.
  3. Add whisked eggs to pan, and evenly distribute.
  4. When the eggs have begun to lightly set, add cheese to one side of the eggs.
  5. Fold the non cheese side over the top of the cheese side.
  6. You can additionally put extra butter on top of the omelette to melt.
  7. Remove from pan when eggs reach desired doneness.
  8. Eggs may be topped with additional garnishes if desired.
